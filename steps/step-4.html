4. Recruit

Consider:

  • Target recruitment to the position.
  • Use multiple platforms.
  • Integrate recruitment with volunteer retention and appreciation.
  • Show what the volunteer experience looks like.
  • Illustrate connection to the mission.
  • Reserve direct appeals for “bleeding-heart” situations.
  • Be clear about how interested people can contact you/get started.
  • Be prepared to communicate with respondents.
