8. Communicate Frequently

Broad Communication:

  • Newsletters
  • Email blasts
  • Social media
  • Signage

Narrow Communication:

  • Email
  • Phone calls
  • One-on-one interactions
  • Birthday cards
  • Photos

With volunteers:

  • Are things going well?
  • Does the volunteer enjoy the work they are doing?
  • Does the volunteer feel needed and productive?
  • Does the volunteer feel connected to your agency’s mission?

With staff managers:

  • Are things going well?
  • Has the volunteer’s work been helpful?
  • Are there any concerns?
  • Has the staff member been communicating with the volunteer?
  • Has the staff member been tracking hours and productivity?
  • Have there been any incidents?
