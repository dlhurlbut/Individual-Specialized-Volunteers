2. Identify Needs and Prepare Staff

Consider:

  • Communicate within your agency!
  • Create a formal mechanism for requesting volunteers.
  • Seek volunteer opportunities that will connect volunteers to your mission.
  • Manage staff expectations regarding:
    • Volunteer recruitment
    • Volunteer performance
    • Volunteer management

Prepare staff to:

  • Communicate frequently with volunteers and volunteer management staff.
  • Have work for the volunteers to do before they arrive.
  • Express appreciation verbally and in writing.
  • Track and report volunteer hours and outputs.
  • Report volunteer incidents.
